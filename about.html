About

My name is Kyle Fallon, and I'm a freshman at Brigham Young University. I am super interested in politics, and I usually try to stay updated on the political news. Since middle school, I've always been interested in political topics like the minimum wage and gun control.

My dad was a Republican, which has influenced my thought process and stance on political issues, but as a party, I do not like the Republican party. I feel that they are wrong on several social issues, and that their political priorities are messed up.

I took a world and U.S. government and politics class last year in high school, which has greatly improved my knowledge on the interworkings of U.S. politics and the politics that goes on in several different countries. One of the countries we studied was Iran, which is why I made a paper arguing for greater efforts of diplomacy with Iran.
